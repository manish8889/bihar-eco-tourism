Adventure & Eco Activities

Immerse yourself in nature with our sustainable adventure experiences led by expert guides and wildlife rangers.

Jungle Jeep Safari

Ride deep into the tigers' kingdom at Valmiki or encounter lion enclosures inside Rajgir's deep green canyons.

Bird Watching

Scan forest canopies and vast river wetlands hosting over 200 species of migratory and domestic birds.

Nature Eco-Walk

Trek down winding deciduous forest pathways rich with fresh oxygen and rare ayurvedic medicinal herbs.

Wilderness Camping

Spend starry nights inside durable glass dome tents along canyon rims or rivers with crackling campfires.

Eco Boating

Paddle down Gandak rivers or float across Ganges streams watching river mammals emerge at sunset.

Sky Cycling

Pedal along steel rope ziplines suspended 100 feet above the valley forests inside Rajgir Safari hills.

Nature Photography

Photograph spectacular limestone gorges, roaring waterfall cascades, and Bengal tigers hiding in tall grass.

Hill Trekking

Hike up historical rocky tracks spanning Nalanda's ancient hills or Kaimur's massive plateau valleys.

Night Forest Safari

Ride under starlight on restricted night safaris exploring active nocturnal owls, civets, and leopards.

Wildlife Showcase

Hover over cards to discover scientific titles and fun biological facts about Bihar's native animals, birds, and aquatic mammals.

Panthera tigris

Royal Bengal Tiger

The apex predator of Valmiki Tiger Reserve. Their population in Bihar has shown a significant positive rise due to tight conservation practices.

Platanista gangetica

Gangetic River Dolphin

Declared the National Aquatic Animal of India. Locally known as 'Susu', they are completely blind and use echolocation to navigate.

Melursus ursinus

Sloth Bear

Highly active in the Bhimbandh and Gautam Buddha sanctuaries. They love feeding on termites and honey from dry deciduous forests.

Panthera pardus fusca

Indian Leopard

Extremely agile climbers inhabiting Rajgir Nature Safari and Kaimur's rocky canyons. Highly elusive and active during nights.

Pavo cristatus

Indian Peafowl

Abundant across all sanctuaries, especially in Rajgir. Their grand courtship dances are a major highlight during the onset of Monsoons.

Buceros bicornis

Great Hornbill

Can be seen in the canopy of Valmiki Tiger Reserve. They play an indispensable role in forest seed dispersal and plant regeneration.

Elephas maximus

Asian Elephant

Frequently migrate through corridors connecting Valmiki to Nepal's Chitwan Park. Symbol of wisdom and majestic forest guardians.

Euthalia aconthea

Baron Butterfly

Found in mango orchards and dense sanctuaries of Bihar. Known for their perfect camouflage, blending seamlessly into green leaves.
